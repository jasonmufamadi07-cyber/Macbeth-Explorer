# Macbeth-Explorer
Ambition. Power. Betrayal. Blood.  Step into the dark world of Shakespeare’s Macbeth with Macbeth Explorer — the app that breaks down the play act by act, scene by scene, in powerful modern English.  Understand the witches’ prophecy. Feel Lady Macbeth’s ambition. Witness Macbeth’s tragic fall.  Whether you’re preparing for exams or exploring.
